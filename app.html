Your one stop add-on for improving results.

Tap into partners' networks to expand your customer base

Trusted by Leading Brands

Key Features

NextBee's cutting-edge referral partner program seamlessly tracks and rewards verified referrals, allowing you to easily build trusted relationships and share revenue growth with partners.

Automated referral tracking to attribute new leads/deals to referring partners

Seamlessly track and reward your partners for referrals with automated attribution and crediting in Salesforce.

  • Instantly captures referral details when a partner submits a new lead
  • Referral widgets on lead/account/contact pages for easy visibility
  • Automated deal association ensures proper partner credit
  • Flexible crediting rules cater to your unique referral structures
  • Real-time dashboards and reports reveal referral metrics and ROI

Customized referral rewards for partners based on deal size/type

Motivate partners to refer their best customers with customized rewards tailored to each deal's value.

  • Easily set up rules to reward on deal size, type, and products
  • Automated calculations determine the right incentive for every referral
  • Choose between fixed rewards, percentage of deal, or tiered payouts
  • Partners are motivated to refer higher value, strategic opportunities
  • Full transparency into reward calculations and payout details

Rewards fulfillment directly within Salesforce

Streamline rewarding B2B partners directly in Salesforce with automated referral payouts integrated into your CRM.

  • Automated reward payments to partners upon referral deal closure
  • Flexible payout options including PayPal, gift cards, and credits
  • Payment details and status automatically tracked in partner records
  • Partners can view reward earnings and payout history within their account
  • Reward fulfillment workflows integrated into the Salesforce system
